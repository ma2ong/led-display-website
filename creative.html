Creative LED Display Overview

Unleash your imagination with limitless design possibilities

Lianjin Creative LED displays push the boundaries of traditional flat-panel displays with flexible modules, curved configurations, and custom shapes. These innovative solutions enable architects, designers, and artists to integrate LED technology seamlessly into buildings, create immersive environments, and develop unique visual experiences that captivate audiences.

Creative Configurations

Endless possibilities for unique visual experiences

Curved Displays

Concave and convex curved displays with customizable radius from 1m to infinity. Perfect for immersive environments and architectural integration.

  • Horizontal and vertical curves
  • Custom radius options
  • Seamless connections

Spherical Displays

Complete 360° spherical LED displays for planetariums, exhibitions, and artistic installations. Available in various diameters.

  • Full sphere or hemisphere
  • Diameter from 1m to 10m
  • 360° content mapping

Wave Displays

Dynamic wave-shaped displays that create flowing, organic visual experiences. Ideal for retail environments and public spaces.

  • Customizable wave patterns
  • Multiple wave frequencies
  • Ceiling or wall mounting

3D Structures

Complex 3D LED structures including cubes, pyramids, and custom geometric shapes for unique architectural elements.

  • Custom 3D modeling
  • Structural engineering
  • Interactive capabilities

Strip Displays

Flexible LED strips for linear installations, edge lighting, and creative outlining of architectural features.

  • Ultra-thin profile
  • Cuttable to length
  • Weatherproof options

Modular Systems

Interlocking modular LED panels that can be arranged in countless configurations for dynamic, changeable displays.

  • Magnetic connections
  • Hot-swappable modules
  • Reconfigurable layouts
