Technical Specifications

Detailed technical information

Pixel Pitch P3.91, P7.81, P10.42, P15.625
Transparency Rate 70% - 90%
Cabinet Weight 12kg/m²
Cabinet Thickness ≤ 10mm
Brightness 1000-4000 nits
Viewing Angle H: 140° / V: 140°
Refresh Rate ≥ 1920Hz
Color Depth 16 bit
Power Consumption Max: 280W/m² / Avg: 93W/m²
Operating Temperature -20°C to +60°C
IP Rating IP43 (Indoor) / IP65 (Outdoor)
Lifespan 100,000+ hours
