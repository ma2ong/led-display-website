Outdoor LED Display Overview

Built to withstand the elements while delivering exceptional visual impact

Lianjin Outdoor LED displays are engineered for superior performance in challenging outdoor environments. With IP65/IP66 protection ratings, high brightness levels up to 10,000 nits, and robust construction, these displays deliver outstanding visibility and reliability for digital billboards, sports venues, transportation hubs, and outdoor advertising applications.

Weather Protection Features

Engineered to withstand extreme weather conditions

IP66 Protection

Complete protection against dust ingress and powerful water jets from any direction. Tested to withstand heavy rain, snow, and extreme weather conditions.

Wide Temperature Range

Operates reliably in extreme temperatures from -40°C to +60°C with advanced thermal management and climate-resistant components.

UV Resistance

UV8-rated materials and coatings protect against sun damage and color fading, ensuring long-term visual performance and structural integrity.

Wind Resistance

Engineered to withstand wind speeds up to 200 km/h with reinforced cabinet structure and secure mounting systems for maximum stability.

Corrosion Protection

Salt spray resistant coating and marine-grade materials provide excellent protection in coastal environments and industrial areas.

Lightning Protection

Built-in surge protection and lightning arresters safeguard the display from electrical storms and power surges, ensuring continuous operation.
