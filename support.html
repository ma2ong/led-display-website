Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Our LED displays come with a comprehensive 3-year warranty covering all components and labor. Extended warranty options up to 5 years are available. The warranty includes free replacement of defective modules and on-site service for major installations.

Firmware updates can be performed using our LED Studio Pro software. Download the latest firmware from our support portal, connect to your display via Ethernet or USB, and follow the step-by-step update wizard. Always ensure stable power supply during updates.

Dead pixels can be caused by various factors. First, check all connections and power supply. Use our diagnostic software to identify the issue. If it's a module problem, individual modules can be replaced without affecting the entire display. Contact our support team for replacement modules.

Indoor displays should be cleaned monthly with a soft, dry cloth. Outdoor displays may require weekly cleaning depending on environmental conditions. Never use water or chemical cleaners directly on the display. Use compressed air for dust removal and specialized LED cleaning solutions when necessary.

Yes, our LED Studio Pro software supports multi-display management. You can control up to 64 displays from a single computer using Ethernet connection. Each display can show different content or work together as one large display wall.

Our displays support all major video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, WMV, and image formats like JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF. For best performance, we recommend H.264 encoded MP4 files. Live streaming via RTMP and NDI is also supported on professional models.
